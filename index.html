FAQ

What's a hackathon?
Who can attend Hack K-State?

Hack K-State is open to all high school, undergraduate, and graduate students. We also accept applications if you have graduated within the last 12 months.

How much does it cost?

It's FREE! Thanks to our sponsors, we'll be providing food, dedicated sleeping areas, and awesome swag all weekend.

Are you providing travel reimbursement?

We can't confirm travel reimbursement right now, but we will be looking at our budget after the hackathon. If you'd like to be considered for travel reimbursement, click the checkbox when you submit your application.

Are there teams?

Yes! Bring yourself and 3 friends! If you don't have a team, but would like to be on one, we'll help you find the team that's right for you!

I don't know how to code.

Hackathons are one of the best ways to learn new things! We'll have student and professional mentors available to answer all your questions!

I'm not a Computer Science major.

Hackathons aren't just for programmers, they're for everyone! Whether you have a cool app idea, can create awesome artwork or music, or don't know what you're good at, there's something for you to build at Hack K-State.
