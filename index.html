About Hack K-State

Hack K-State is an official student run competition team at Kansas State University founded in 2017. Every year the team organizes a hackathon and sponsors official university teams to travel and participate at other hackathons.

This year for the Hack K-State hackathon we're expecting 200 high school, undergraduate, and graduate students from around the world! Students show up on Friday night and dream up an awesome website, mobile app, or robot and then get to build it over the weekend. At the end of the hackathon everyone gets to share their creations!
