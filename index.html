About Hack K-State

Hack K-State is an official student run competition team at Kansas State University founded in 2017. Every year the team organizes a hackathon and sponsors official university teams to travel and participate at other hackathons.

This year for the Hack K-State hackathon we're expecting 200 high school, undergraduate, and graduate students from around the world! Students show up on Friday night and dream up an awesome website, mobile app, or robot and then get to build it over the weekend. At the end of the hackathon everyone gets to share their creations!

FAQ

What's a Hackathon?

Check out this awesome video from our friends at MLH!

Who can attend Hack K-State?

Hack K-State is open to all high school, undergraduate, and graduate students. We also accept applications if you have graduated within the last 12 months.

How much does it cost?

It's free! Thanks to our sponsors, we'll be providing food, swag, and sleeping areas all weekend!

Are there travel reimbursements?

We'll be looking at our budget after the hackathon and will potentially do travel reimbursements. If you'd like to be considered, please check the travel reimbursement box in your application.

Are there teams?

Yes! Teams can have up to four people. If you don't have a team but want to be on one, we'll have a team building activity before the hacking begins on Friday.

I'm not a Computer Science major.

Awesome! Hackathons are for everyone, not just programmers. Whether you have a cool app idea, can create awesome artwork or music, or don't know what you're good at, there's something for you to build at Hack K-State.

I don't know how to code.

Hackathons are one of the best ways to learn new things! We'll have student and professional mentors available to answer all your questions!

What am I going to make?

You can make anything you want! Checkout this DevPost page, for some cool projects others have built!

Are you allowing walk-ins?

At this point in time we will not be accepting walk-ins. If you have been accepted please fill out the confirmation so that we know you're coming!

What should I bring?

  • School/Government ID
  • Laptop and Charger
  • Water Bottle and Toiletries
  • Clean Clothes
  • Sleeping Bag/Blanket

What should I NOT bring?

  • Weapons
  • Drugs
  • Anyting Illegal
  • Previous Projects

What if I want to shower?

Showers will be available at the K-State Rec for periods of time during the hackathon.
